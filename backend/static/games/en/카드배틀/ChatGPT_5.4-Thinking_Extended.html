Soft Card Battle

Combine attack, block, and heal cards to defeat the Jelly King.
Block resets at the start of your next player turn.

Core Rules
• Recover 3 energy at the start of each turn
• Draw 3 random cards
• Cards cannot be used if they cost more than your energy
• When you end your turn, the enemy acts and the next turn begins
